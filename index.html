RDZ Quality Homes and Construction

About
Founded in 2008, RDZ Quality Homes and Construction is a family-owned business dedicated to delivering reliable, high-quality remodeling and construction services. With over 30 years of experience, our licensed general contractor leads every project with a commitment to craftsmanship, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
Services
We specialize in full home remodels and offer a wide range of services, including framing, kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, roofing, electrical, plumbing, and more. No matter the size of the job, we approach every project with transparency, strong communication, and a focus on getting the work done quickly and professionally.
Rentals
In addition to our remodeling services, we also offer rental homes, giving families and individuals access to comfortable, well-maintained living spaces built with the same care and standards we bring to our construction work.
At RDZ Quality Homes and Construction, we believe in doing things the right way—providing dependable service, honest pricing, and exceptional results that homeowners can trust for years to come.
